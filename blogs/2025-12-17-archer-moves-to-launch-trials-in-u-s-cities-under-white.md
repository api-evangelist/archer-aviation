Archer is partnering with cities across the country to submit multiple applications to launch initial air taxi operations under the White House’s eVTOL Integration Pilot Program (eIPP), a first-of-its-kind public-private initiative led by the U.S. Department of Transportation (DOT) and Federal Aviation Administration (FAA) to support the safe integration of air taxis in the U.S. beginning as soon as next year.
